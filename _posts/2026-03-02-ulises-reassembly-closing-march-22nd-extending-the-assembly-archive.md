The Ulises: *Reassembly* exhibition closes this month on March 22 but we have great news! The *Assembly* archive will remain on view in our project space through the end of the year, with a short hiatus in April. We will continue to offer programs and activations around the archive throughout the year.

*Reassembly* brings together three independent art publishers: [Can Can Press](https://cancanpress.com/) (Mexico City, Mexico), [Further Reading](http://further-reading.club/) (Bandung, Indonesia), and [Ruth van Beek ](https://www.ruthvanbeek.com/)(Koog aan de Zaan, Netherlands), whose contributions to *Assembly* inspired a closer examination of their practices. This new presentation invites visitors to engage with their work to gain insight into the processes, materials, and concepts that shape each practice. 

Exhibited alongside the cataloged *Assembly* archive, *Reassembly* extends an ongoing conversation about the labor and care that shape the field of independent art publishing.

The exhibition considers the material, conceptual, and social dimensions of publishing, and the forms of attention, collaboration, and repetition that define the work behind the work. Reassembly invites new ways of engaging with the publishers’ methods, materials, and research, foregrounding the relationship between what we do and how we do it.
