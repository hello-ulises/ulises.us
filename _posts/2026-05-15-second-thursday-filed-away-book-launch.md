This Thursday, join us for the launch of Filed Away, a collaborative publication organized by the Tyler Printmaking MFA program, exploring the collections we keep and interrogating the collections kept about us. This handmade volume features the work of twenty-two artists from around the country and includes screen printing and risograph and a unique double spiral bound spine.  



Participating Artists:  Katie Garth, Corinne Teed, Savannah Bustillo, Amze Emmons, Lenora Yerkes, Michael Vasquez, Olivia Fredericks, Lu Walstad, Hannah Moog, Rylie Kelley, Juliette Boulay, Hester Stinnett, Jill Adler, Annie Klein, Olivia Smith, Josh Kery, Eliza Clifford, Christina Kang, Jordan Divers, Rachel Avallone, Sabrina Mellado, Lila Shull, Mary Climes, Jess Santana, Emmett Merrill
