This month, [Screen o Series](https://screenoseries.com/) (S.o.S.) will feature It Never Was You (2003), directed by Sandeep Mehta for German electronic artist Thomas Brinkmann and Rave Macbeth (2001), a loose adaptation of Shakespeare's Macbeth set in an early 2000’s rave scene, directed by Klaus Knoese.



S.o.S. is a bi-monthly film screening and fundraising series hosted by artists Greyory Blake, Masina Frank, and Yōsuke Araki. The hosts trade off selecting a feature (max 2hr) and a short (max 30m). 



This event is pay-what-you-can. All proceeds this month will be donated to [Juntos](https://www.vamosjuntos.org/donate-to-juntos).
