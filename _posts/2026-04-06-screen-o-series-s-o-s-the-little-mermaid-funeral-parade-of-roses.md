Screen o Series (S.o.S.) is a bi-monthly/occasional film screening at Ulises hosted by Greyory Blake, Masina Frank, and Yōsuke Araki. We trade off selecting a feature (max 2’) and a short (max 30”). Pay what you will. All proceeds go to a rotating set of causes that befit thematic or pressing/relevant needs.



This month, S.o.S. will feature *The Little Mermaid* (1968), a Soviet animated adaptation of Hans Christian Andersen's 1837 fairy tale directed by Ivan Aksenchuk, and *Funeral Parade of Roses* (1969), a tender portrayal of trans-being in shadow of the Vietnam War, directed by Toshio Matsumoto.



*\*\*Content Warning: Funeral Parade of Roses contains themes and images depicting suicide, self-harm, self-mutilation, sex, blood, violence, drugs, alcohol, incest, and nudity. \*\**



All proceeds will be donated to [Morris Home](https://www.rhd.org/support-our-work/).
