Saturday, June 20, 7pm - 8pm [RSVP](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/1991152769534?aff=oddtdtcreator)



Celebrate the vinyl release of nature gurl, the new poetry and jazz album from Philly-based band Yolanda Wisher & The Afroeaters. nature gurl revolves around recorded words from Wisher’s mother, whose deep connection with animals shaped the album’s spirit, exploring shared frequencies and honoring the wild creature within us all. The evening will feature live performances by Wisher with Afroeaters Paul Giess and Mark Palacio as well as The Song Poet. 



About Yolanda Wisher & The Afroeaters: 

Yolanda Wisher & The Afroeaters is a Philadelphia-based music collective. Their albums Doublehanded Suite (2022) and nature gurl (2026) bring the group’s blend of jazz, soul, and spoken word into recorded form. The band has performed at the Philadelphia Museum of Art, The Barnes Foundation, the Kimmel Cultural Campus, and WRTI’s Live Sessions studio, creating immersive soundscapes that traverse the continuum of poetry and song.
