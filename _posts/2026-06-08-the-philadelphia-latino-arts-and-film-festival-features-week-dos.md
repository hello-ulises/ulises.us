Saturday, June 6, 2026, 11 - 4pm

Now in its 15th season, the [Philadelphia Latino Arts and Film Festival](https://www.phlaff.org/) (PHLAFF) was established in 2012 as the Philadelphia Latino Film Festival. PHLAFF has become the Greater Philadelphia region’s only festival showcasing the extraordinary and innovative work of emerging and established Latine/x/a/o filmmakers.

This year’s festival programming brings together local and international films to Philadelphia for the first time, May 24 - July 5. Each film selection taps into personal stories and experiences of culture, societal differences, social change, and more.
