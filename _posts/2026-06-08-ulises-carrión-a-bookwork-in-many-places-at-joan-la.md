Ulises is proud to announce participation in Ulises Carrión, a bookwork in many places at [JOAN](https://www.joanlosangeles.org/), featuring selections from the Ulises: Assembly archive, running from May 24 to  August 9, 2026. 

JOAN is a Los Angeles-based nonprofit art space for exhibitions, performances, screenings, and discursive events.
