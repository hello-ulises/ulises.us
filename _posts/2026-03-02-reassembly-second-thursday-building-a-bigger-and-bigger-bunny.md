Have you seen Ulises and *Reassembly* exhibiting artist [Ruth van Beek](https://www.ruthvanbeek.com/)’s very big bunny recently? This Second Thursday, we invite you to come for a drink and continue building the bunny with us.



Expanding her *Assembly* submission, *The Largest Rabbit (Assembly / Catalog No. 0085)*, into a wall-sized work, visitors are encouraged to engage directly with her process, scanning copies of rabbits from van Beek’s archive, printing, cutting, and pasting them into a single, larger rabbit. 



Snacks, beverages and materials provided.
